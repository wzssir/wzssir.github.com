  • 1. How can I manage my previous orders?

    There is an entry “My eMark” in the upper right corner of the navigation bar. Click here and you will jump to the order management page.

    You may view your previous orders by order number, time, status, applicant, and mark etc. You may download confirmation sheet of application, check status of order and reason of failure, or delete invalid order here.

  • 2. What if I have to amend my order/application?

    An order is generated once you confirm all the application information. So if you have to amend your application after the order is generated, you may withhold the payment and delete this order, and then start a new application.

    Please note that an order/application cannot be amended or withdrawn once the payment is settled, and neither the official fee nor the attorney fee is refundable under such circumstance. So please be careful and cautious when you are to confirm an order. Should you need to officially withdraw the application from China Trademark Office, please contact us at info@emarkchina.com for detailed procedure and cost.

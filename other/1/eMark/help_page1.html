  • 1. How does it work to file a trademark application through emarkchina.com? Is it cumbersome?

    There are roughly THREE steps to file a trademark application through emarkchina.com, i.e. (1) become a user, (2) fill in trademark application form, and (3) pay. Each of these steps requires no more work than the traditional way in which you may choose to file an application.

    Firstly you have to create an account to become a user. Use your primary e-mail to create one, set password, and get verified so you will be accessible to our full service.

    Secondly you have to prepare accurate information regarding your mark and the applicant to fill in the online Application Form. Supporting materials e.g. certificate of identity/incorporation of the applicant, specimen of your mark, and power of attorney (available on emarkchina.com) are also required which you may deliver by uploading. It should be noted that emarkchina.com is quite user-friendly to assist you completing those.

    Thirdly you may pay through multiple options e.g. via Paypal, Western Union, or Wire Transfer if you prefer to the traditional payment through banks.

  • 2. What if I forgot my password?

    Click the “Forgot password” and you will jump to another page to find it back through your e-mail which you have used for creating the account.

  • 3. How can I manage my account? Not all the account information is editable. Why?

    There is an entry “My eMark” in the upper right corner of the navigation bar. Click here and you will jump to the account management page.

    Some of the account information is not editable, e.g. the primary e-mail based on which a user has created the account, the company name, type of user etc. Such information is very essential for emarkchina.com to recognize or manage the corresponding account, and changing it or part of it may cause problem in our record. Therefore, if a user do need to change such information, please contact us at info@emarkchina.com to ensure proper solution.

    Another important entry, “Frequent Applicant” is not editable because any change to it may result in inconsistence of information in the previous applications under the name of this applicant, and involve procedure of change of the bibliographic data of the official application/registration and related fees. We strongly recommend you to contact us at info@emarkchina.com if your applicant(s) is/are involve(s) in such changes.

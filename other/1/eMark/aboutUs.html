About us

  • eMarkChina

    eMarkChina Filing System Co., Ltd. was established by a group of the earliest intellectual property experts and practitioners in China who are inspired by the Internet operation model and deliver the intellectual property law services over the Internet.
    eMarkChina is staffed with trademark experts, experienced attorneys, lawyers, and in-house investigators, and is supported by the leading trademark database in China. With the coordination by the visible hand of staff and the invisible hand of technologies, E-Mark takes the lead to transform the traditional model of intellectual property law service.

  • What we do?

    Online Self-Service Filing:
    It is a ground-breaking reform of eMarkChina to launch the Online Filing Platform to streamline the trademark applications. A client only needs to type in the application information, upload relevant material, and pay on line to complete applications. The platform not only seeks to buy time for the clients in thousands of new applications filed in China every day, but also saved repeated procedure and excessive labor, rendering the online filing cost more competitive than the traditional means.

    Other Basic Applications:
    In addition to the convenient online filing, E-Mark is also open for traditional trademark applications, registration of copyright and domain names etc. In light of any specific circumstances, E-Mark also provides pre-stage strategy tailored to the client’s actual needs.

    Complicated Cases:
    As a matured IP agency with rich experience, E-Mark has strong presence in dealing with complicated cases such as reviews of refusal or opposition, litigation, infringement and anti-counterfeiting making use of various available venues---administrative raids, customs seizures, civil and administrative litigation, and combination of several as is tailored to the demand of the clients.

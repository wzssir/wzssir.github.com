Account Application Order Fee

  • 1. Why was the order status updated into “verifying” after I have settled my payment? How soon will my application be submitted once I have paid?

    Once your action of payment is recorded in the system, it will verify with the bank or the receivable accounts whether the payment is made in full amount, which will take days. For Paypal, the payment will be advised immediately; for Western Union, it may take 3-7 weekdays to verify the payment; for Wire Transfer, 7-10 weekdays. We shall submit your application once we have verified you payment.

  • 2. May I withdraw my application and have my payment refunded?

    We are afraid not. Due to systematic reasons, any payment is not refundable once it is settled.

  • 3. Is it cheaper to file multi-class application than single-class application based on the same items of goods/services?

    No, on the basis of same items of goods/services, the official fees by the two systems are equal. The official fee is calculated on the basis of number of classes and the number of items in excess of 10 under each class, not on the basis of application. For details, please refer to the Fee Rules.

  • 4. I have made online payment and confirmed that the amount has just been transferred from my payable account, but the order status still shows “unpaid”. What can I do with that?

    Sometimes the status update might be delayed due to heavy traffic of your network. Please wait for a while and refresh to see if the status changes.

    If the “unpaid” status remains, the payment is possibly stuck in the third party payment platform, i.e. the Paypal or the Western Union. Please contact the customer service of those third party payments to clear such trouble. Also feel free to contact us at info@emarkchina.com if we are of any assistance.
